Eastern Communication

Eastern Communications (ETPI), is a connectivity solutions company providing an extensive portfolio of services that include Internet, Data, Voice and Managed Services. ETPI pioneered the first communications service in the Philippines when it was commisioned by the Spanish Government to provide the country's first telegraphic services.

Today, the company has evolved into a full service provider of of world-class telecommunications services allowing it to be continue to be the solutions partner of choice to the biggest industry players in the country.

ETPI continues to be a company that is comitted to the quality of the products and services it provides. As the first Philippines telecommunication company to recieve an ISO certification for its business operations, the company promises to deliver excellent products and services where customers can be assured of reliable business-grade connectivity solutions 24/7.

ISO Certification

Eastern Communications marked a milestone in the Philippine telecommunications history by being the first telecommunications to attain the prestigious ISO 9001 certification. It reflects the company's good management practices, quality processes, and customer-focused service delivery systems.

The benefit that ISO certification brings to Eastern Communications' corporate clients is the aasurance of quality in its telecommunication solutions. Carrying the ISO 9001:2008 mark also signifies to Eastern's clients that the company adheres to quality standards and world-class processes.

To get the certification, each division had to pass trough a series of audits and inspection of their business processes and quality assurance program. The audit was done by TUV SUD, a leading one stop global solution provider for product quality testing , inspection, training and certification.

The audit showed that the corporate goals and strategies are communicated troughput the organization and each division defines how they contribute to achieve the company's objectives. The inspection also revealed that the Eastern's business processes is sufficiently documented, deployed and complied. More importantly, the deliverables of employees are communicated, measued, and tracked.

Eastern Communication now stands for quality within the telecommunication's industry in the country. The ISO certification testefies to the company's ability to deliver superior sutomer satisfaction trough quality service. Importantly, it reflects the company's vision of being the most preffered telco wherever its services are present and the company's culture of service excellence and operational responsiveness.
