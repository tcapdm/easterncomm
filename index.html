ABOUT US

(ETPI)
Eastern Communications

is a connectivity solutions company providing an extensive porfolio of services that include Internet, Data, Voice and Managed Services. ETPI pioneered the first communications service in the Philippines when it was commisioned by the Spanish Government to provide the country's first telegraphic services.

Internet Services

Eastern Communications offers a wide range of Internet products to choose from depending on customer’s needs. Its Internet Direct Service is a dedicated Internet Service that runs on leased line technology providing customers with best route to content, via Eastern Communications’ multiple peering partners in the Philippines and abroad.

Eastern Communications also offers DSL, providing its customers quality broadband access that is constantly available allowing the continuous exchange of data. It is a reliable and cost-effective alternative to IDS suitable for smaller offices or for work from home employees.
